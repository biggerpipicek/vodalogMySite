Vodalog is a Logistic company located in the Czech Republic that specializes in handling of Consumer Electronics.

Vodalog meets the needs and resolves the daily challenges our customers face in the international supply chain. Our experience and expertise in caring goods safely, securely and efficiently has earned Vodalog an outstanding reputation as a distinguished logistics company. Our Strategy is to serve our Customers with High Quality Solutions through Innovation, Competence, Flexibility and Reliability. Vodalog produces the best service, of the right quality and the right price, and remains Flexible to adapt to all your needs and unique situations.

We take care of your goods 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year, also beyond the normal daily working hours, with an extra charge. Every day, every hour, our goal is to match customer and business needs.

Motivated and dedicated Professional Team with exceptional Multilingual Skills and Logistics Expertise will meet the needs of our customers. To achieve this goal Our Staff is Permanent Employed so they can build relationships and gain invaluable experience in dealing with each customer.

Vodalog has a Share Capital of 120 000 CZK fully paid and our Bank is Raiffeisen Bank. On the Pricelist section you can find our Logistic Services Agreement.
