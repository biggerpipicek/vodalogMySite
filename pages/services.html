Our High Quality Solutions are tailored specifically for each Customer in order to meet his Needs. With Highly Trained and Experienced employees, Vodalog handles complete supply chain activities from procurement to arrival and management of the goods. The Warehouse Manager ensures that the requirements of the Management Systems are met and continuously improved.


Our main services are:

Unloading the Goods
Inspection Report and Photos
Full RF Scanning
X - RAY Goods Control
Stock Management
ON HOLD Service
Re-Packing and Re-Labeling
Loading the Goods

Accepting and Releasing the Goods is done on Monday – Friday from 8:00 till 17:00, minimum time for Notice is 12 hours in advance.

We take care of your goods 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year, also beyond the normal daily working hours, with an extra charge. Every day, every hour, our goal is to match customer and business needs.
